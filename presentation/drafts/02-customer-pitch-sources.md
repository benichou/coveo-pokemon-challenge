## 4 · Benefits Delivery Modernization (BDM) Programme

| Source | URL | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Auditor General Report 8 — BDM Programme** | https://www.oag-bvg.gc.ca/internet/English/att__e_44349.html | **Primary source** for BDM phasing, costs, timeline risks |
| **HUMA Committee Briefing Binder · Feb 5, 2024** | https://www.canada.ca/en/employment-social-development/corporate/reports/committe-binders/2024-feb-5-huma-beech.html | BDM status mid-2024 |
| **March 2025 Minister Jobs & Families briefing** | https://www.canada.ca/en/employment-social-development/corporate/reports/esdc-transition-binders/2025-march-transition-binder-mackinnon.html | Latest BDM milestones + EI roadmap |
| **May 2025 Minister briefing** | https://www.canada.ca/en/employment-social-development/corporate/reports/esdc-transition-binders/2025-may-transition-binder-hajdu.html | OAS BDM platform launched 2025; EI as next focus |
| **600K OAS clients onboarded announcement** | https://www.canada.ca/en/employment-social-development/news/2023/12/600000-old-age-security-clients-successfully-onboarded-to-the-new-common-benefit-delivery-platform.html | First OAS BDM release (June 12, 2023) |
| **Capital Hill Group analysis (CHG 2026)** | https://capitalhillgroup.ca/the-benefits-delivery-modernization-programme/ | Independent third-party landscape |
| **EI Monitoring 2024 — Ch 4** | https://www.canada.ca/en/employment-social-development/programs/ei/ei-list/reports/monitoring2024/chapter4.html | EI program operations including 6 RPA bots |

**Key BDM facts (verified 2026-06-05 via Auditor General Report 8 + briefing binders):**
- BDM unifies **OAS, GIS, CPP, EI** on one common delivery platform · target completion **2030**
- **3 phases**: OAS (Phase 1) → EI (Phase 2) → CPP (Phase 3)
- **Phase 1 (OAS)**: First release June 12, 2023 (600K Foreign Benefits clients) · major new payment system launched March/April 2025 · ~7M OAS clients to transition
- **Phase 2 (EI)**: Migration scheduled **2025-2028** across multiple releases · **First EI release (Benefits Estimator) launched beta in 2024** · **Build & implement phase began 2025** with second release (Compassionate Care Benefits for the Self-Employed)
- Auditor General caveat: *"This migration will be complex because of the significant number of system changes needed to incorporate policy updates while also implementing service transformations that have already been delayed. This complexity and the ambitious scope pose a risk of further delays."*
- Driven by aging systems: CPP 20yr · EI 50yr · OAS 60yr · "complex, fragmented, prone to errors"
- Cost trajectory: $1.75B (2017 est.) → $2.5B (April 2022 est., +43%) · $817M spent since 2017 · $2.2B Treasury Board approvals over 10+ years
- Staffing risk: "limited pool of candidates" with required technical skills
