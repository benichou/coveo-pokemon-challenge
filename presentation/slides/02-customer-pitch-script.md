## 3 · Before vs After
"What changes for the officer, day to day.
**Today** — open a case in the fifty-year-old system, keyword-search the policy database hoping you typed the right section, switch tabs, re-paste citations by hand. Edge case? Hold it, escalate.
**With Coveo** — the detail page loads in under a second: summary, policy, similar cases. Edge case? Type the question — grounded answer with citations in two to three seconds, auto-captured into the notes. **The senior-officer answer becomes the every-officer answer.**
Same story in the call centre — except today the citizen's on the line hearing 'let me check that…' With AI-Relevance the agent types, gets an answer in seconds. **First-contact resolution lifts.**"
